Privacy Policy for Heading Highlighter

Heading Highlighter does not collect, store, or transmit any personal or sensitive user data.

The extension operates entirely within the user's browser and only processes webpage content locally in order to identify and highlight heading elements (H1–H6). This information is not stored, shared, or sent to any external servers.

User preferences, such as selected heading highlights, are stored locally using Chrome's storage API and are not accessible outside the user's browser.

This extension does not:
- Track browsing history
- Collect personal information
- Use analytics or tracking tools
- Share data with third parties
